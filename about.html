About the Author

Jose Lucio Lima Guerra is a statistician and quantitative geneticist working at Enza Zaden, an international vegetable-breeding company. His work focuses on experimental design, breeding analytics, and the development of AI-assisted decision tools for researchers.

AI Ensemble is part of his independent academic exploration into how uncertainty, statistical mechanics, and ensemble reasoning can shape next-generation scientific tools.
