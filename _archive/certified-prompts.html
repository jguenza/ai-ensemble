Certified Prompts

Certified Prompts are prompts that AI Ensemble has evaluated and confirmed to produce stable, scientifically credible, and reproducible experimental designs. They pass tests for:

  • clarity of scientific intent
  • absence of hidden confounders
  • statistical and experimental design correctness
  • stability under probabilistic evaluation (e.g., Gibbs sampling)
  • alignment with accepted statistical models (mixed models, ANOVA, GLM, etc.)

Only prompts that consistently produce valid designs across many clean-state runs are certified.
