Certified Prompts

Certified Prompts are prompts that the AI Ensemble system has evaluated for:

  • clarity of scientific intent
  • statistical credibility
  • absence of hidden confounders
  • alignment with reproducible study design principles
  • stability under probabilistic evaluation (e.g., Gibbs sampling)

Only prompts that consistently lead to stable and interpretable experimental designs are certified. As the project grows, more prompts will be added to this list.

Submit Your Prompt for Certification

You may submit your own prompt for certification. The evaluation process examines whether your question:

  • produces statistically sound designs when interpreted by the agent
  • avoids ambiguity or hidden assumptions
  • remains stable across multiple clean-state reasoning cycles
  • matches recognized scientific frameworks (e.g., ANOVA, GLM, REML, experimental design theory)
