Where We Are

AI Ensemble is evolving into a system for AI-assisted experiment design. Early testers want an agent capable of producing statistically grounded designs (factorial, randomized, mixed models, augmented, longitudinal) while explaining the reasoning behind each choice.

Some real research questions involve trade-offs and decisions that standard filtered models refuse to answer. AI Ensemble explores a low-filter regime so users can receive honest, structured, and complete experiment proposals.

The system currently has two layers:

  • Front-end agent: interacts with users to produce experiment structures, confounder maps, hypotheses, sample-size strategies, and statistical models.
  • Ensemble agent: reads anonymized logs, detects recurring experiment structures, and evaluates credibility using probabilistic methods including Gibbs sampling.

The next development phase includes automatic aggregation of experiment templates and probabilistic ranking of alternative designs.
