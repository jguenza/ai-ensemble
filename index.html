The Project

AI Ensemble is a framework that validates classical scientific research through reproducible code and delivers that verified knowledge through Generative AI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). Our objective is to create a trustworthy, expert-aligned computational layer that scientists can rely on for experiment design, hypothesis generation, and probability-based reasoning. Our Approach Reproduce and validate scientific articles We critically review scientific publications and reproduce their results using rigorous statistical and computational methods. The goal is to confirm the correctness and reproducibility of the findings. Register validated code in a curated RAG knowledge base Verified code—together with expert annotations, assumptions, and constraints—is stored in a structured knowledge base. This ensures transparency, traceability, and long-term reproducibility. Integrate validated resources with Generative AI Curated code and domain insights are combined with modern AI capabilities to provide: Precise coding guidance Reliable statistical advice Genetic analysis strategies grounded in validated methods Enable probability-driven, hypothesis-oriented research By anchoring AI responses in peer-reviewed computational evidence, researchers can: Design robust experiments Explore hypotheses using simulation Apply probabilistic thinking with confidence Experimental Design Agent The first product of the AI Ensemble project is the Experimental Design Agent. This agent: Leverages validated simulation code as its computational backbone. Helps users obtain maximum value from their data by referencing verified statistical workflows. Supports hypothesis generation, experimental planning, and uncertainty quantification using expert-curated resources. Ensures recommendations remain aligned with established scientific principles and formally validated implementations. Role of Validated Code Validated code is central to AI Ensemble: It is reproduced, reviewed, and approved by subject-matter experts. It forms the technical foundation of our RAG database. It constrains and informs AI outputs, preventing hallucinations and ensuring reproducible recommendations. It connects AI reasoning directly to authoritative computational sources used to replicate scientific work. As a result, the platform delivers AI-augmented scientific support that is transparent, defensible, and grounded in real computational evidence. Value Proposition AI Ensemble produces large volumes of reliable statistical and genetic-analysis code—generated by AI but supervised, corrected, and validated by experts. This code becomes reusable infrastructure that accelerates a wide range of scientific activities: Experimental design Statistical modeling Genomic prediction and simulation Hypothesis testing Probability-driven decision-making Researchers benefit from expert-verified, AI-supported workflows that drastically reduce the time needed to design and evaluate scientific experiments.
